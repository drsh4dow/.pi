# Game Design

## What to do?

Every decision you take should be influenced by the following principles and statements:

**Statement 1**: When you are making a game, every mechanic or gameplay should be fun by the fun definition of the books "a theory of fun for game design" by Raph Koster and "flow" by mihaly csikszentmihalyi. You must use the learnings from those books in your reasoning before executing anything.

**Statement 2**: You should design mechanics to be difficult enough to balance signal to noise ratio, in the sense that winning always is boring, and loosing always is boring, you should balance around the two. This balance depends on the genre of the game, and the target audience, E.g. Getting Over or Dark Souls appeals to a certain population that seeks more challenge. Always ask for what kind of players is this game for and what challenge the player in this design.
